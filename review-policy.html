Review Policy

Home / Review Policy

Every manuscript submitted to Dynamic Agriculture undergoes the following review process before publication:

  • Initial screening: The editorial office checks scope, formatting, completeness, and plagiarism.
  • Editorial review: Manuscripts are reviewed by subject-matter editors for technical accuracy, originality, clarity, and relevance to readers.
  • Decision: Accept, minor revision, major revision, or reject — usually communicated within 7–10 days.
  • Revision: Authors must submit revised manuscripts within the stipulated time addressing all comments.
  • Final decision: All decisions are made by the core editorial committee and are final. Individual editors cannot be contacted or blamed for any decision.
  • Retraction: Articles found to involve unethical practices after publication may be retracted, and authors may be blacklisted.
