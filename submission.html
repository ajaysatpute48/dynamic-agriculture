Ready to submit? Read the guidelines below, then upload your manuscript through the Submission Portal at the bottom of this page. There are no submission or processing charges.

1. About Dynamic Agriculture

Dynamic Agriculture is a peer-reviewed open-access e-magazine dedicated to disseminating knowledge in agriculture and allied sciences. It serves as a platform for scientists, researchers, extension workers, students, and practitioners to share innovations, discoveries, success stories, and developments that contribute to the advancement of the agricultural sector. The magazine is published in digital format and is freely accessible to readers worldwide.

2. Scope & Manuscript Categories

Dynamic Agriculture welcomes manuscripts across all areas of agriculture and allied sciences, including but not limited to:

  • Crop science, agronomy, and soil science
  • Horticulture, floriculture, and landscape management
  • Animal husbandry, veterinary science, and fisheries
  • Agricultural engineering and post-harvest technology
  • Agroforestry and natural resource management
  • Agricultural extension, rural development, and policy
  • Food science, nutrition, and food safety
  • Biotechnology, genetics, and plant breeding
  • Climate-smart agriculture and sustainability

The following categories of manuscripts are considered for publication:

Popular Articles

Original, evidence-based popular science articles.

Success Stories

Field-level innovations, farmers' experiences, and best practices.

News

Coverage of conferences, workshops, and events.

Extension Articles

Advisory content for practitioners.

3. General Conditions for Submission

  • Manuscripts must be original and must not have been previously published in any form, nor be under consideration for publication elsewhere.
  • All forms of plagiarism, self-plagiarism, data fabrication, and falsification are strictly prohibited. Submitted manuscripts will be subjected to plagiarism screening. Authors will be solely responsible for any breach.
  • The corresponding author must ensure that all co-authors have reviewed, approved, and consented to the submission.
  • Any conflict of interest — financial, institutional, or personal — must be declared by the authors at the time of submission.

4. Manuscript Preparation Guidelines

4.1 Language and General Format

  • Manuscripts must be written in clear, simple English that is understandable to readers across all domains of science and agriculture.
