How to Submit Your Article

Step 1

Prepare Manuscript

Download the article template from the Download page and prepare your manuscript as per author guidelines below.

Step 2

Email Submission

Send your manuscript (MS Word file) along with the duly filled declaration form to dynamicagriculture@gmail.com.

Step 3

Review & Publication

The editorial team reviews your article within 7–10 days. On acceptance and fee payment, the article is published in the upcoming issue with a certificate.

Author Guidelines

  • Manuscript should be original and plagiarism-free (below 20% similarity). See our Plagiarism Policy.
  • Language: English only. Length: 1500–3000 words (popular article).
  • Format: MS Word (.doc/.docx), Times New Roman 12 pt, 1.5 line spacing.
  • Structure: Title, Author(s) with affiliation and email, Abstract (150–250 words), Keywords (4–6), Introduction, Main content with headings, Conclusion, References.
  • Maximum 4 authors per article.
  • Tables and figures must be numbered, captioned, and cited in the text.
  • References in APA style.
  • Certificate of publication is issued to all authors.
