Plagiarism Policy

Home / Plagiarism Policy

Dynamic Agriculture follows a strict anti-plagiarism policy to maintain the quality and ethics of publication:

  • All manuscripts are screened using plagiarism-detection software before review.
  • Overall similarity must be below 20%, with no single source exceeding 5%.
  • Self-plagiarism (reusing one's own published text without citation) is treated the same as plagiarism.
  • Manuscripts exceeding the similarity limit are returned to authors for revision or rejected outright.
  • If plagiarism is detected after publication, the article will be retracted, the authors notified, and their names may be barred from future submissions. Penalties may apply as per the magazine's policy.
  • Authors are fully responsible for the originality of their submitted content and for obtaining permission for any copyrighted material used.
